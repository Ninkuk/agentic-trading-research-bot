# CRML — Critical Metals Corp. — 2026-07-22

## 1. Verdict and thesis

**Kill-thesis verdict: FLAWED** (7 load-bearing conditions: 1 refuted, 6 UNKNOWN, 0
verifiable). The bull thesis — 92.5%→100% of Tanbreez, one of the largest licensed
heavy-rare-earth deposits outside China, re-rating when the end-2026 feasibility study
validates a fraction of the PEA's $3B NPV — rests on an economic study management itself
calls "obsolete," a schedule the company's own slippage record refutes, and a governance
structure that concentrates every transaction around one person. The deposit is real and
the macro tailwind is real; nothing connecting them to the $1.01B market cap is
verifiable today.

Phase 0 note: this is a pre-revenue mine developer (TTM revenue $769K, FCF -$32.3M,
4 employees). The user explicitly chose the full asset-story analysis over the fast kill.

## 2. Business

**Created (prospective):** Tanbreez, southern Greenland — S-K 1300 initial resource
45Mt @ 0.4% TREO with ~27% heavy REO share, hosted in a 4.7Bt kakortokite unit (the
headline "4.7 billion tonnes" is host rock, not resource). Holds an exploitation
license; low natural radioactivity is the genuine differentiator — the neighboring
Kvanefjeld project was killed by Greenland's 2021 uranium ban while Tanbreez got
licensed. At-surface deposit, fjord port access, planned dry magnetic separation.
Secondary: Wolfsberg lithium (Austria, BMW offtake, EIA litigation pending) — being
shed via the July 8 strategic review of "non-core assets."

**Captured (planned):** sell 100% of planned concentrate under four frameworks — Eucor
10%, ReAlloys 15%, Romanian JV 50% (mine-to-magnet, partner carries CRML's refinery
half), Saudi JV 25% (term sheet; partner-carried refinery). Terms are "long-term
market-based" — no disclosed price floors.

**Protected:** a licensed, low-uranium, heavy-rich deposit cannot be copied — a real
moat *if and only if* extraction is economic, which is exactly the unverified condition.

exists that its own management stands behind.** The March 2025 PEA (NPV ~$3B pre-tax at
12.5–15% discount, IRR ~180% — a promotional-grade figure; feasibility-grade mining
